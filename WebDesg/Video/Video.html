How to Embed a Video in Your Website

Step 1: Find Your Video

Go to your preferred video hosting site and find the video you want to embed.

Step 2: Get the Embed Code

Click the 'Share' button and then 'Embed' to get the HTML code you can use.

Step 3: Copy the Embed Code

Copy the entire embed code provided by the video hosting service.

Step 4: Embed the Video

Paste the copied embed code into your HTML file where you want the video to appear.
